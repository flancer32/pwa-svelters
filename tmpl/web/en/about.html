About NutriLog

My name is Alex Gusev. I'm a web developer from Latvia with over 25 years of experience. NutriLog is my personal experiment in integrating AI into everyday life. It started from a practical need — I used GPT chat to track my weight but struggled with the lack of memory and context. That led me to build a system where GPT can "remember" the user and their goals through external storage and a connected interface.

NutriLog stores key data — weight, diet, goals — and makes it available directly within the conversation. Instead of forms and buttons, users interact naturally with the assistant. The system is based on the model Custom GPT → Actions → Tools and is implemented using TeqFW — a platform reflecting my development philosophy: modularity, JavaScript at all levels, flexible architecture, ease of maintenance, and LLM readiness.

I was inspired by my friend Dmitry, who made real progress through a low-calorie diet. I realized that success comes not only from AI support, but also from the feeling of being part of a community — real people facing the same challenges. That’s why NutriLog is not just a tool, but an attempt to build infrastructure that connects such users.

I don't position myself as a blogger or influencer. I'm a developer who built a solution for myself and is happy to help others. If you’re looking for a similar setup tailored to your needs, I’m open to collaboration.
