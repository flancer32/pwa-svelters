Send Feedback — Right from the Conversation

NutriLog is a powerful extension for GPT chat that helps you structure your nutrition data, track calorie intake, and manage your weight. GPT amplifies your potential — NutriLog amplifies GPT.

You don’t need to fill out forms or write emails to share your thoughts about NutriLog. If something feels off, unclear, or frustrating, you can just say so — during your regular conversation with the Assistant.

The Assistant understands your intent, translates your message into English if needed, and sends it to the NutriLog team. It’s part of how we use real-time human–AI interaction to improve the system together.

Here’s how it works:

  • 🗣️ You say something like: “No me gusta el resumen de alimentos — ¿puedes enviar eso como comentario al equipo?”
  • 📩 We receive your message in English — ready to review and act on it.
