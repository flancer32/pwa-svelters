Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. NutriLog respects your choices and complies with privacy regulations such as the GDPR. This policy explains how we collect and use your information, and how you can manage your preferences.

  • Information We Collect: We collect personal data such as your name, email address, and usage data to operate the NutriLog service.
  • Consent and Tracking: We use Google’s Consent Mode to respect your privacy preferences. Non-essential services such as analytics and advertising are disabled by default and only activated if you explicitly give consent.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time by visiting your user profile. This will immediately deactivate all non-essential tracking.
  • How We Use Your Information: Your data is used to provide and improve our services, support your health goals, and ensure your account’s security.
  • Data Protection: We apply strict security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access control. However, no system can be completely secure.
  • Data Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except when legally required or when using trusted infrastructure providers under strict data agreements.
  • Your Rights: You may access, update, export, or delete your personal data at any time. These options are available from your user profile or upon request.

Cookie Policy

NutriLog uses cookies to ensure basic site functionality and to remember your privacy preferences. We do not set analytics or advertising cookies unless you explicitly grant consent via our cookie banner.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality, such as login sessions and security. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how users interact with NutriLog. These are only enabled after you grant consent.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to optimize our ads and measure conversions. These are disabled by default and only enabled after consent.
  • Cookie Storage: Your consent choice is stored in your browser using local storage and cookies. You can clear or update your preferences at any time.
  • Managing Cookies: You can manage or withdraw your consent anytime by visiting the profile page.

For any questions or concerns, please contact us.
